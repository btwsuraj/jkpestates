Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to your most pressing real estate queries.

Do you handle legal verification of properties?

Absolutely. Property transparency is our highest priority at Jay Keshav Property. We perform rigorous due diligence and title verification on every residential and commercial property. We ensure there are no hidden legal disputes, unpaid property taxes, or builder liabilities before we ever suggest a property to you.

Do you assist Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) with property investments?

Yes, we provide specialized end-to-end NRI services. Managing property from abroad can be incredibly difficult. From identifying high-yield properties to handling banking compliance, taxation processes, remote registry procedures, and managing the property locally, we act as your trusted, boots-on-the-ground representatives in Maharashtra.

Will you help me find a tenant after I buy an investment property?

Yes! Finding the property is just the first step in your investment journey. Our comprehensive Property Management service includes aggressively marketing your flat or shop, performing deep background checks on potential tenants, drafting secure, bulletproof rental agreements, and ensuring timely rent collection month after month.

Why is Vasai-Virar and Nalasopara considered a top investment zone?

The Western line, particularly the Mira-Bhayandar to Virar corridor, is experiencing massive infrastructure upgrades. With continuous municipal road expansions, improved railway connectivity, and planned commercial hubs, property values are steadily rising. This ensures excellent capital appreciation and remarkably high rental yields compared to the oversaturated, highly expensive city centers in South Mumbai.
